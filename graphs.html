The data displayed on this page has been directly sourced from the 2021 UNEP Food Waste Index Report, which can be found here.

When examining these graphs depicting both food waste per capita and total food waste, viewers can derive several noteworthy conclusions regarding the intricate relationship between these two metrics. The graphs enable a direct comparison of absolute and relative values, shedding light on which categories or regions contribute the most to the overall food waste. Simultaneously, by delving into the per capita perspective, viewers gain insights into the average waste generated by individuals within different categories or regions, offering a normalized view that considers population differences.
